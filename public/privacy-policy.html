Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2024

1. Introduction

Rundee Bot ("the Bot") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

2. Data We Collect

The Bot collects and stores the following information to provide its services:

2.1 Server and Channel Data

  • Guild ID (Server ID): Identifies the Discord server
  • Channel IDs: Stores the channels where notifications should be sent

2.2 Meeting Information

  • Meeting titles: The title/subject of scheduled meetings
  • Meeting dates and times: When meetings are scheduled
  • Participants: Discord user mentions and role mentions associated with meetings
  • Reminder settings: When reminders should be sent
  • Recurring meeting settings: For repeating meetings (daily, weekly, monthly patterns)

2.3 Configuration Data

  • Language preference: Your server's language setting (English/Korean)
  • GitHub repository: Repository URLs for webhook integration

2.4 What We DON'T Collect

  • We do not collect personal messages or direct messages
  • We do not collect message content outside of bot commands
  • We do not collect email addresses or other personal information beyond Discord IDs
  • We do not track user behavior or analytics

3. How We Use Your Data

We use the collected data solely for the following purposes:

  • To send meeting reminders at scheduled times
  • To deliver GitHub webhook notifications to your specified channels
  • To maintain your server's configuration preferences
  • To provide bot functionality as requested through commands

4. Data Storage

Data is stored locally in a SQLite database on our servers. We take reasonable measures to protect your data, but no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

5. Data Retention

Data is retained for as long as:

  • The Bot is installed in your server, or
  • As needed to provide the service

When you remove the Bot from your server or delete meeting data through bot commands, associated data is removed from our systems.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your data to third parties. Data is only shared in the following circumstances:

  • With Discord's API (as required for bot functionality)
  • With GitHub's API (only if you configure GitHub webhook integration)
  • If required by law or legal process

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access: View what data we have stored (use bot commands like /list-meetings and /channel-status)
  • Delete: Remove meetings using /delete-meeting command
  • Modify: Update meeting information using /edit-meeting command
  • Remove Bot: Remove the Bot from your server at any time, which will stop future data collection

8. Children's Privacy

The Bot is not intended for users under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13. If you believe we have collected data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

9. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify users of significant changes by updating the "Last updated" date at the top of this policy.

10. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy or wish to request deletion of your data, please contact us through:

  • Discord: Contact the bot developer
  • GitHub: Open an issue on the project repository
